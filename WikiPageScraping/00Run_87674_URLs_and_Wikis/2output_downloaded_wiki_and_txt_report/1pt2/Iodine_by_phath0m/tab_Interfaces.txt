          <p>Interfaces in Iodine are defined using the <code>interface</code> keyword. Only function prototypes are allowed inside the interface body. An inteface may not extend another class or interface.</p>
<p><strong>Example</strong></p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-go"><pre><span class="pl-k">interface</span> <span class="pl-v">IMyInterface</span> {
    <span class="pl-k">func</span> <span class="pl-c1">aFunction</span> ();
}</pre></div>
