          <p>The Color data type represents a color.  Within a contract configuration, they may be specified as an HTML color value, which is the character <code>#</code>, followed by 6 hex characters.</p>
<p>Example:</p>
<ul>
<li>#FF0000 - Red</li>
<li>#00FF00 - Green</li>
<li>#0000FF - Blue</li>
</ul>
