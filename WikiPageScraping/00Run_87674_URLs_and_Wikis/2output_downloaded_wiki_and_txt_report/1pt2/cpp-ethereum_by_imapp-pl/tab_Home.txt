          <p>Ethereum is a Decentralised Consensus-based Deterministic Transaction Resolution Platform.</p>
<p>In addition to the original whitepaper, it currently exists as a <a href="http://gavwood.com/Paper.pdf" rel="nofollow">formal specification</a> and a number of proof-of-concept codebases, of which this is one.</p>
<p>If you like the idea of developing on Ethereum, the best development platform currently is Ubuntu 14.04 with the QtCreator IDE. If you would like to know more about building Ethereum for your platform you'll want to inspect:</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="internal present" href="/imapp-pl/cpp-ethereum/wiki/Building-on-Ubuntu">Building on Ubuntu</a></li>

</ul>
