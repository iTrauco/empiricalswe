<p>You can use your SmartSwitch to trigger Events on IFTTT and you can turn on and off LEDs or change colors (SmartSwitch Mobile only) based on IFTTT events. This section describes how to setup your SmartSwitch and IFTTT. This feature is based on the spark cloud IFTTT integration, details can be found at <a href="https://www.spark.io/ifttt" rel="nofollow">Spark.io IFFTT</a>.</p>
