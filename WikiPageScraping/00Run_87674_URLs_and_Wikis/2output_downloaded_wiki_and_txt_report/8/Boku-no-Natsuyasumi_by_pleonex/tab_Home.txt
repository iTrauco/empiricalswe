<p><strong>Boku no Natsuyasumi</strong> is a <em>Play Station 1</em> (PS1 / PSX) game developed by <em>Millennium Kitchenx</em> in 2000. Its genre is <em>adventure</em> and it's the first part of a serie of games for several devices like <em>PS3</em> and <em>PSP</em>. All of them are only in <strong>japanese</strong> and as far as I know there is not any past or future translation. In this repository there is information about the <em>PSP</em> version.</p>
