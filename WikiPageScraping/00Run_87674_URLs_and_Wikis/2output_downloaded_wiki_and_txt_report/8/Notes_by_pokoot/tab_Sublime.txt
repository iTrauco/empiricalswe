2. Then go to Tools &gt; Record a Macro to start recording.
3. Press `End` to go to the end of line.
4. Press `;` and hit Enter
5. Stop recording the macro by going to Tools &gt; Stop Recording Macro
6. You can now test your macro by Tools &gt; Playback Macro (optional)
