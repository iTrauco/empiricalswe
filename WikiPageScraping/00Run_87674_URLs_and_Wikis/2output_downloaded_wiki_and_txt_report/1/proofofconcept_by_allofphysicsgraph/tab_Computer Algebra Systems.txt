<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#mathml-instead-of-latex" id="user-content-mathml-instead-of-latex"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>MathML instead of Latex</h1>
<p>Instead of storing equations as Latex, store them as MathML (<a href="http://www.w3.org/Math/" rel="nofollow">http://www.w3.org/Math/</a>). Then</p>
<ul>
<li>convert from MathML to Latex for rendering in graph: <a href="http://xsltml.sourceforge.net/" rel="nofollow">http://xsltml.sourceforge.net/</a>
<ul>
<li>better: MathML directly to pictures (SVG, PNG)
<ul>
<li><a href="http://sourceforge.net/projects/pmml2svg/" rel="nofollow">http://sourceforge.net/projects/pmml2svg/</a></li>
<li><a href="http://sourceforge.net/projects/jeuclid/" rel="nofollow">http://sourceforge.net/projects/jeuclid/</a></li>
<li><a href="http://sourceforge.net/projects/svgmath/" rel="nofollow">http://sourceforge.net/projects/svgmath/</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>convert from MathML to CAS input
<a href="https://www.google.com/search?q=mathml+to+latex" rel="nofollow">https://www.google.com/search?q=mathml+to+latex</a>
</li>
</ul>
<p>It's not reasonable to have a human generate the MathML manually</p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MathML#Example_and_comparison_to_other_formats" rel="nofollow">https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MathML#Example_and_comparison_to_other_formats</a>
Thus we need Latex to MathML</li>
<li><a href="http://www.mathtoweb.com/cgi-bin/mathtoweb_home.pl" rel="nofollow">http://www.mathtoweb.com/cgi-bin/mathtoweb_home.pl</a></li>
</ul>
