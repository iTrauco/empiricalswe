<h1>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#assignments" id="user-content-assignments"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Assignments</h1>
<p>In <strong>ASSESSMENT &gt; Assignments</strong> teachers can create assignments for students.</p>
<p>If the course has <a href="USERS.Groups.en">groups</a>, you can choose to list either the assignments for the groups to which you belong (<em>Only my groups</em>) or all the assignments for the course (<em>All groups</em>).</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#new-assignment" id="user-content-new-assignment"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>New assignment</h2>
<p>To <strong>create</strong> a new assignment, you must click on the <em>New assignment</em> icon (plus sign) at the top left of the box or the button <em>New assignment</em>. A form appears with the following fields:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Title</strong>. A title for the assignment.</li>
<li>
<strong>Start date</strong>. Date and time when the assignment will begin to be available.</li>
<li>
<strong>End date</strong>. Date and time when the assignment will be unavailable.</li>
<li>
<strong>Upload files?</strong>. Optionally you can indicate the name of a folder in which each student will upload his/her files for this assignment.</li>
<li>
<strong>Description</strong>. Optional description about the assignment.</li>
<li>
<strong>Groups</strong>. Students to whom the assignment is assigned:
<ul>
<li>Any student from the whole course</li>
<li>Only students from selected groups</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>When you fill in the fields, you must press the button <em>Create assignment</em>. The new assignment will be listed.</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#edit-assignment" id="user-content-edit-assignment"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Edit assignment</h2>
<p>To <strong>remove</strong> an existing assignment, click on the <em>Remove</em> icon (red cross). To effectively remove the assignment you will have to confirm the deletion by clicking on the <em>Remove assignment</em> button.</p>
<p>To <strong>hide</strong> an existing assignment, click on the <em>Hide</em> icon (eye). To <strong>unhide</strong> a hidden assignment, click the <em>Unhide</em> icon (strikethrough eye).</p>
<p>To <strong>edit</strong> an existing assignment, click on the <em>Edit</em> icon (pencil). A form identical to the one for <a href="#new-assignment">creating a new assignment</a> will appear. When the editing is finished, click on the <em>Save</em> button.</p>
<p>To <strong>print</strong> an existing assignment, click on the <em>Print</em> icon (printer).</p>
