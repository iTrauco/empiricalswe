<p><a href="USERS.Teachers.en">Teachers</a> and <a href="USERS.Administrators.en">administrators</a> will receive <a href="USERS.SignUp.en">enrolment requests</a> as <a href="MESSAGES.Notifications.en">notifications</a>.</p>
<p>If your <a href="PROFILE.Session.en#role">current role</a> is a teacher or administrator you can administer such requests in <strong>USERS &gt; Requests</strong>. You can filter the requests by choosing:</p>
<ul>
<li>The scope:
<ul>
<li>System</li>
<li>Country</li>
<li>Institution</li>
<li>Centre</li>
<li>Degree</li>
<li>Course</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>The requested roles:
<ul>
<li><a href="USERS.Students.en">students</a></li>
<li><a href="USERS.Teachers.en">teachers</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>You will see a <strong>list</strong> with all the requests, sorted from the most recent to the oldest. In each row of the list the following information is displayed:</p>
<ul>
<li>Course</li>
<li>Current number of teachers in the course</li>
<li>Requester (the user who want to sign up in the course)</li>
<li>Requested role</li>
<li>Date and time of the request</li>
</ul>
<p>and two buttons:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Register</strong>: green button, to accept the enrolment request</p>
<p>In this case the requester' record card and a button to confirm the registration will be displayed. Depending on your <a href="PROFILE.Session.en#role">current role</a> and the role requested, you will be able to change the request role.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Reject</strong>: red button, to reject the registration</p>
</li>
</ul>
