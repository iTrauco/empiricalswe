<h1>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#indicators-of-courses" id="user-content-indicators-of-courses"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Indicators of courses</h1>
<p>The <strong>ANALYTICS &gt; Indicators</strong> option displays information on the degree of compliance with certain quality indicators by the courses that are hosted on the platform. In <a href="COURSE.Information.en">COURSE &gt; Information</a> there is a link to the indicators fulfilled by the current course.</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#course-selection" id="user-content-course-selection"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Course selection</h2>
<p>In this option it is possible to select the courses whose indicators are to be displayed. To do this, in first place, select the appropriate <strong>scope</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>System</li>
<li>Country</li>
<li>Institution</li>
<li>Centre</li>
<li>Degree</li>
<li>Course</li>
</ul>
<p>If the chosen scope is <em>System</em>, you can choose the <strong>type of degree</strong>.</p>
<p>You can also filter the courses by those that are taught by teachers of a certain <strong>department</strong>.</p>
<p>Finally, you can choose to display all the courses or only those that satisfy a certain number of indicators, between 1 and 5.</p>
