<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#contact" id="user-content-contact"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Contact</h3>
<p>Detonates the missile when it hits any surface: the ground, a tank, a player...</p>
<p><strong>Ingame description:</strong><br/>
<code>"This fuse triggers upon direct contact against solid surfaces."</code></p>
<hr/>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#optical" id="user-content-optical"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Optical</h3>
<p>Detonates the missile when any solid object is caught within a line traced in front of the missile.</p>
<p><strong>Ingame description:</strong><br/>
<code>"This fuse fires a beam directly ahead and detonates when the beam hits something close-by."</code></p>
<p><strong>Configuration:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Distance:</strong> the length of the line traced in front of the missile.  Solid objects will only be detected when they are closer than this maximum range.</li>
</ul>
<hr/>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#radio" id="user-content-radio"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Radio</h3>
<p>Detonates the missile when the missile's target comes close enough, in any direction.<br/>
This fuse requires Wire or Radar guidance - it will not work without one of these to provide a target.</p>
<p><strong>Ingame description:</strong><br/>
<code>"This fuse tracks the guidance module's target and detonates when the distance becomes low enough."</code></p>
<p><strong>Configuration:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Distance:</strong> the maximum detonation distance, measured from the centre of the missile to the centre of the target.  The target must be at least this close to cause detonation.</li>
</ul>
<hr/>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#timed" id="user-content-timed"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Timed</h3>
<p>Detonates the missile when it hits a solid object, or after it has been flying for a user-defined length of time.</p>
<p><strong>Ingame description:</strong><br/>
<code>"This fuse triggers upon direct contact, or when the timer ends."</code></p>
<p><strong>Configuration:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Trigger Delay:</strong> the amount of seconds that the missile must spend flying before it detonates.</li>
</ul>
