<p>##APIO DONGLE</p>
<p>The master of the Apio Mesh network</p>
<p>Apio Dongle is a microcontroller designed to be the coordinator of an Apio Mesh network. It comes as an USB Dongle with a pre-installed firmware that creates, controls and maintains the network.</p>
<p>If you connect Apio Dongle to any PC or single-board computer (Raspberry PI, Beagleboard, etc.) with Apio OS installed in, you can have a personal IoT platform that you can control from Smartphone, Tablet and PC.</p>

<p>##APIO GENERAL</p>
<p><em>A general purpose node for the Apio Mesh network</em></p>
<p>Apio General is an electronic board with an integrated wireless communication channel that can be programmed with the Arduino IDE, so it's fully compatible with their community libraries. Thanks to Apio OS you can control your Apio General from Smartphone, Tablet and PC. Furthermore, to simplify the Apps development in our Cloud, we build Apio SDK: an integrated software development tool that help you code and create awesome Apps.</p>

<p>##APIO PI SAVER</p>
<p>Apio Pi Saver is a shield that will safely shutdown your Raspberry Pi in the event of unexpected power outages. The integrated Super capacitor guarantees up to a minute of power forever with no maintenance.</p>
<p>The board features an ATtiny85 microcontroller which handles the communication with the Raspberry Pi through its GPIOs and the events of connecting and disconnecting the power. In particular, we provide a python script that will launch the shutdown of the Raspberry Pi as soon as it receive the signal of disconnection form power source. Furthermore, our board will automatically reactivate the Raspberry Pi when the power source reconnect.</p>
