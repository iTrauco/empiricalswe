          <p>In order to prevent confusion, I have added terminology used in the code and its descriptions.</p>
<p>Game Completion Terms</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>All</strong> - All of the users games</li>
<li>
<strong>Unfinished</strong> - Games the user hasn't completed the single player campaign of.</li>
<li>
<strong>Finished</strong> - Games the user finished the single player campaign of, but didn't get all the achievements of.</li>
<li>
<strong>100% Complete</strong> - Games the users have finished the single player campaign of and gotten all the achievements of.</li>
</ul>
<p>Storage Clases</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Platform</strong> - the service that you log into to play the game</li>
<li>
<strong>Game</strong> - the game itself</li>
</ul>
<p>List Terms</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Platform ID</strong> - the ID given to the platform</li>
<li>
<strong>Sort Type</strong> - The sorting type used in the list. Ex: Name, Hours Played, Recently Played, etc.</li>
<li>
<strong>Sort Direction</strong> - The direction of the sorting.</li>
<li>
<strong>Sort Ascending</strong> - The direction of the sorting, true if Ascending or false if Descending</li>
<li>
<strong>Filter Type</strong> - The game completion status that the list is filtered on. Ex: Finished, Unfinished, 100% Complete</li>
</ul>
