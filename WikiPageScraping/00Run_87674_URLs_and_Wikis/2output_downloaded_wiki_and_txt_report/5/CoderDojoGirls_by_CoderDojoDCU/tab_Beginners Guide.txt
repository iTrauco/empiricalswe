          <p>Welcome to the CoderDojoGirls wiki!</p>
<p>First day at CoderDojo?</p>
<p>We'd like you to follow these steps (and don't be afraid to call over a mentor for help!):</p>
<ol>
<li>Connect to our internet wifi. Its usually called 'Be Cool' or 'CoderDojo2' and the password is <em>coderdojo</em> or <em>coderdojodcu</em>
</li>
<li>Does your laptop use Windows or is it an Apple Mac laptop?</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Windows</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Download Notepad++ <a href="http://notepad-plus-plus.org/download" rel="nofollow">http://notepad-plus-plus.org/download</a>
</li>
<li>Download Google Chrome browser if its not already installed. You can use Firefox if you prefer.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Apple Mac</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Download TextWrangler <a href="http://www.barebones.com/products/textwrangler/download.html" rel="nofollow">http://www.barebones.com/products/textwrangler/download.html</a>
</li>
<li>Download Google Chrome browser if its not already installed.</li>
</ul>
<ol start="3">
<li>Get the latest code from a usb stick from a mentor or download from our github page (a mentor will show you where). You need to save it on your laptop. I recommend creating a new folder on your desktop called CoderDojoGirls and saving it in that folder.</li>
<li>If its a .zip file you will have to right click the file and 'extract to' the CoderDojoGirls folder on your desktop.</li>
<li>Have fun and get coding! Don't be afraid to ask questions :)</li>
</ol>
