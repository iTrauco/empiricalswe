## English Guide
<a name="user-content-download"></a>
#### Download
Download [Omega](https://github.com/DonZooles/project-omega/releases)
<a name="user-content-locate"></a>
#### Locate
Extract the zip-file and move 'project-omega' into the Mumble skin-folder
* Example - C:\Program Files\Mumble\skins\project-omega
<a name="user-content-activate"></a>
#### Activate
1. Open Mumble and go to `Configure &gt; Settings &gt;` 'User Interface'.
2. Click on `Look and Feel &gt; Skin &gt;` 'Browse...' and...<br/>
2.1 Lite: ...choose the '../activate_omega-lite.qss'.<br/>
2.2 Dark: ...choose the '../activate_omega-dark.qss'.<br/>
3. Apply the changes and restart Mumble. (for Icons)

## Deutsche Anleitung
<a name="user-content-herunterladen"></a>
#### Herunterladen
Lade [Omega](https://github.com/DonZooles/project-omega/releases) herunter
<a name="user-content-lokalisieren"></a>
#### Lokalisieren
Entpacke den Ordner und schiebe 'project-omega' in den Skin-Ordner von Mumble
* Beispiel - C:\Program Files\Mumble\skins\project-omega
<a name="user-content-aktivieren"></a>
#### Aktivieren
1. Öffne Mumble und gehe zu `Konfiguration &gt; Einstellungen &gt;` 'Benutzerinterface'.
2. Klicke auf `Aussehen &gt; Skin &gt;` 'Durchsuchen...' und...<br/>
2.1 Lite: ...wähle die Datei '../activate_omega-lite.qss'.<br/>
2.2 Dark: ...wähle die Datei '../activate_omega-dark.qss'.<br/>
3. Wende die Änderungen an und starte Mumble neu. (Für Icons)

<a name="user-content-different-language"></a>
## Different Language
<p>Change the language of your Mumble:</p>
<ol>
<li>Open Mumble and go to <code>Configure &gt; Settings &gt;</code> 'User Interface'.</li>
<li>Click on <code>Look and Feel &gt; Language &gt;</code> and choose 'UK English (en_GB)'</li>
<li>Apply the changes and restart Mumble.</li>
</ol>
