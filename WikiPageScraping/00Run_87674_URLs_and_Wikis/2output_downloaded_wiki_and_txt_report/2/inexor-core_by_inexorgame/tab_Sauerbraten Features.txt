<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#game-features" id="user-content-game-features"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Game Features</h2>
<ul>
<li>Old-school fast &amp; intense gameplay (read: similar to Doom 2 / Quake 1).</li>
<li>23 multiplayer gameplay modes, mostly available in teamplay variants as well: ffa (free for all, deathmatch alike), instagib, efficiency, tactics, capture (domination/battlefield style), CTF (capture the flag), protect (one teammate have to carry the flag), hold (hold a randomly spawning flag for a specific time to score), collect (kill enemies and collect their skulls) and coop edit (create maps together!).</li>
<li>7 weapons: double barrelled shotgun, rocket launcher, machine gun, rifle, grenade launcher, pistol and chainsaw.</li>
</ul>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#engine-features" id="user-content-engine-features"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Engine Features</h2>
<ul>
<li>6 directional heightfield in octree world structure allowing for instant easy in-game geometry editing (even in multiplayer, coop edit).</li>
<li>Rendering engine optimized for high geometry throughput, supporting hardware occlusion culling and software precomputed conservative PVS with occluder fusion.</li>
<li>Lightmap based lighting with accurate shadows from everything including mapmodels, smooth lighting for faceted geometry, and fast compiles. Soft shadowmap based shadows for dynamic entities.</li>
<li>Pixel and vertex shader support, each model and world texture can have its own shader assigned. Supports normal and parallax mapping, specular and dynamic lighting with bloom and glow, environment-mapped and planar reflections/refractions, and post-process effects.</li>
<li>Loading of md2/md3/md5/obj/smd/iqm models for skeletal and vertex animated characters, weapons, items, and world objects. Supports animation blending, procedural pitch animation, and ragdoll physics for skeletally-animated characters.</li>
<li>Simple stereo positional sound system.</li>
<li>Particle engine, supporting text particles, volumetric explosions, soft particles, and decals.</li>
<li>3d menu/gui system, for in-world representation of choices. <strong>(removed from Inexor due to refactoring of the entire UI system)</strong>
</li>
</ul>
