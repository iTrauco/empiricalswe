<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#download-and-install-gnu-gettext" id="user-content-download-and-install-gnu-gettext"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Download and install GNU Gettext</h3>
<p>For windows users, go to <a href="https://mlocati.github.io/articles/gettext-iconv-windows.html" rel="nofollow">https://mlocati.github.io/articles/gettext-iconv-windows.html</a> and download the GNU Gettext installer of your choice (for other platforms, get it here: <a href="https://www.gnu.org/software/gettext/" rel="nofollow">https://www.gnu.org/software/gettext/</a>).</p>
<p>Install the program to a location, preferably one without spaces in the file-path (just makes your life easier).</p>
<p>In this example: <code>C:\Programs\GNUGettext</code></p>
<p>Take note of the install-location!</p>

<h1>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#getting-the-uskyblock-source-code" id="user-content-getting-the-uskyblock-source-code"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Getting the uSkyBlock source-code</h1>
<p>Open IntelliJ IDEA, and chose the <code>Checkout from Version Control</code> option on the start-screen (or get to it from the <code>File &gt; New &gt; Checkout existing</code>).</p>
<p>Punch in the URL: <code>https://github.com/rlf/uSkyBlock.git</code> and chose a sensible location for your local version of the project, and press Clone.</p>
<p>IDEA will now download the complete project to your local harddrive, and open it for you.
Just click next through most of the wizards you will encounter.</p>
<p>Make sure you point IDEA to the proper JDK (downloaded above).
Once opened, IDEA will download all dependencies for uSkyBlock - so be patient at this stage.</p>
