<p>Required compiler &amp; linker options:</p>
<ul>
<li>-D_WIN32_WINNT=0x0501 (required for networking)</li>
<li>-DWINVER=0x0501 (required for networking)</li>
<li>-static-libgcc</li>
<li>-static-libstdc++</li>
<li>-std=c++11</li>
<li>including all dependency files and libraries</li>
</ul>
<p>For more info about the two -D options, see <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/desktop/aa383745%28v=vs.85%29.aspx" rel="nofollow">this msdn article</a>.</p>

<p>Compiling <a href="https://github.com/sndnv/Syn/wiki/Connection">Connection</a>:<br/>
<code>g++ -D_WIN32_WINNT=0x0501 -DWINVER=0x0501 -static-libgcc -static-libstdc++ -c -g -Wall -Iexternal -std=c++11 "build/NetworkManagement/Connections/Connection.o.d" -o build/NetworkManagement/Connections/Connection.o src/NetworkManagement/Connections/Connection.cpp</code></p>
<p>Linking <a href="https://github.com/sndnv/Syn/wiki/Connection">Connection</a> &amp; <em>main</em>:<br/>
<code>g++ -D_WIN32_WINNT=0x0501 -DWINVER=0x0501 -static-libgcc -static-libstdc++ -o dist/sync_server build/NetworkManagement/Connections/Connection.o build/main.o -Lexternal/boost_lib -Lexternal/cryptopp_lib -lboost_thread-mgw48-mt-1_55 -lboost_system-mgw48-mt-1_55 -lboost_regex-mgw48-mt-1_55 -lws2_32 -lwsock32 -lcryptopp -lboost_filesystem-mgw48-mt-1_55</code></p>
