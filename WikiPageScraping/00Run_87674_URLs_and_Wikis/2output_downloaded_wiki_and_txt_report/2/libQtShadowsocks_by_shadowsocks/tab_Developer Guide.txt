          <p>The namespace of <code>libQtShadowsocks</code> is shortened as <code>QSS</code>, which stands for <strong>Q</strong>t<strong>S</strong>hadow<strong>S</strong>ocks.</p>
<p>The easiest and also the most recommended way to utilise this library is to use <code>Controller</code> to configure/start/stop shadowsocks services. <code>Controller</code> can be used either in <code>local</code> or <code>server</code> mode.</p>
<p>The sub-project <code>shadowsocks-libqss</code> is used as a demonstration to show you how to use <code>libQtShadowsocks</code>.</p>
<p>Do bear in mind that all dependencies of <code>libQtShadowsocks</code> will be passed into your project, which includes <code>QtCore</code>, <code>QtNetwork</code>, and <code>Botan</code>.</p>
