<p>Thought this might be useful. Here are a few code words for Terminal, iPython, etc.</p>
<ul>
<li>Ls: lists directory content</li>
<li>Cd: goes to the specified directory</li>
<li>Pwd: displays path</li>
<li>Cd..: goes up one directory level</li>
<li>Mkdir: creates a directory</li>
<li>Nano: opens a text editor</li>
<li>Rm: removes or deletes a directory or file</li>
<li>WC: word count, with the modifier -L it counts the lines of text</li>
<li>Cat: concatenate</li>
<li>Sort: sorts and selects</li>
<li>Head: shows the first lines of a text file</li>
<li>Tail: shows the last lines of a text file</li>
<li>./: runs a program</li>
</ul>
