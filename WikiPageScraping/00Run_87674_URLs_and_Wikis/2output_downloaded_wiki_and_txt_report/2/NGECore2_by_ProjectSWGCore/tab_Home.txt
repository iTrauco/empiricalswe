          <p>Welcome to the ProjectSWG NGECore Wiki.</p>
<p>The Open Source version of ProjectSWG is restricted to 30 minutes uptime and up to 5 connections to ensure that the community is not split between several small servers.  This restriction will not be lifted, it is also not legal to remove this restriction for anyone (see the engine's license for more legal information).</p>
<p>The engine (NGEngine) is a closed source library and is only licensed for use with this project.  The core (NGECore2) is licensed under the GPL license.</p>
