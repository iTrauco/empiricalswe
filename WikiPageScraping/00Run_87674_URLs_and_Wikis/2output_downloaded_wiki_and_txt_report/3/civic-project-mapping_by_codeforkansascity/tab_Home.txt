          <p>##Community KC personas, goals and tasks.</p>
<p>##Primary goal for this app -- connecting citizens
(or "Tinder for Projects").</p>
<p>###Personas</p>
<ul>
<li>Shelly (primary) – aspiring citizen, 28, tech savvy</li>
<li>Jon – 60, old hippie, Caucasian, organizer</li>
<li>Gloria -75, African American, community matriarch</li>
<li>501C3 - Community organization, nonprofit</li>
</ul>
<p>###Shelly's tasks:</p>
<ul>
<li>review projects in the neighborhood to see the fit, to join;</li>
<li>see project volunteering needs (volunteers, what kind?));</li>
<li>star project to show support/preferences;</li>
<li>comment, coordinate projects.</li>
</ul>
<p>###Jon, Gloria tasks:</p>
<ul>
<li>apply for a grant from Community Fund;</li>
<li>keep project needs, description, events up to date;</li>
<li>organizing events;</li>
<li>print project as a flier;</li>
<li>print list of all projects in the neighborhood (with contact, brief description, map).</li>
</ul>
<p>###501C3 tasks:</p>
<ul>
<li>add projects to the list</li>
<li>keep an eye on the projects.</li>
</ul>
