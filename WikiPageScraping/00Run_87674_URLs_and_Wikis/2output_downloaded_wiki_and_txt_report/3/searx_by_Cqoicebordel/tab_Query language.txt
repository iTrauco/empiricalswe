          <p>Searx allows you to modify the default categories, engines and search language via the search query.</p>
<p>Category/engine prefix: <strong><code>!</code></strong></p>
<p>Language prefix: <strong><code>:</code></strong></p>
<p>Abbrevations of the engines and languages are also accepted.
Engine/category modifiers are chainable and inclusive (e.g. <a href="https://searx.me/?q=%21it%20%21ddg%20%21wp%20qwer" rel="nofollow"><strong><code>!it !ddg !wp qwer</code></strong></a> - search in IT category <strong>and</strong> duckduckgo <strong>and</strong> wikipedia for <code>qwer</code>).</p>
<p>See the <a href="https://searx.me/preferences" rel="nofollow">/preferences page</a> for the list of engines, categories and languages.</p>
