<p>How come the PhoneGap Developer App won't connect to the local server started by the PhoneGap Desktop App?</p>
<p><em>The computer with PhoneGap Desktop App and the mobile device with PhoneGap Developer App must be on the same network.</em></p>
<p><em>Your network may have the PhoneGap Desktop App's server port blocked; <a href="https://github.com/phonegap/phonegap-app-desktop/issues/162">this could be the result of network security settings, firewall, VPN or being on a corporate network</a>.</em></p>
