          <p>$F4 <strong>ENTRY</strong> - This record is used in a run-time library dictionary. It contains a 2-byte number and an offset followed by a label. The number is the segment number. The label is a code-segment name or entry, and the offset is the relative location within the load segment of the label.</p>
