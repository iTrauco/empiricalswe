          <p>$E0 <strong>ALIGN</strong> - The operand is a <code>NUMLEN</code> byte number indicating the even byte boundary to align to. Alignment to a page boundary would appear, with <code>NUMLEN=4</code>, as <code>E0 00 01 00 00</code>.</p>
