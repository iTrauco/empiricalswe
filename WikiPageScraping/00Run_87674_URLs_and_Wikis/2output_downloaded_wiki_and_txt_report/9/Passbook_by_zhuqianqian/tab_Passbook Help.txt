<p><strong>Introduction</strong></p>
<p>Passbook is a handy app that can save your credential information securely (using AES 256 bits key encryption). It integrates automatic synchronization (using your Google Drive*) and can import data from other apps, so never worry about data loss. Passbook is designed for simplicity, high security and great flexibility. You will find it extremely easy to use.</p>
<p><strong>Install Passbook</strong></p>
<p>Passbook for android: Download it from <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.z299studio.pbfree" rel="nofollow">Google Play Store</a> for free.</p>
<p>Passbook for iOS: Available soon.</p>
