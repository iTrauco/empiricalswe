<p>Provides an optimized version of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuwahara_filter" rel="nofollow">Kuwahara filter</a>. This implementation was chosen because it actually shows a flaw in the Snapdragon 805 / Adreno 420 GPU. When turning the filter on / off with a Nexus 6 notice the rendering flaw. This doesn't occur on the Adreno 320/330 or the Tegra K1.</p>
