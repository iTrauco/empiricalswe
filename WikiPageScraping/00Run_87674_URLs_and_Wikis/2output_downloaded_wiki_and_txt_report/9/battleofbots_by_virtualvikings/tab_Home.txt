          <p>Battle of Bots is a strategy game developed by Virtual Vikings. The main goal of the game is to battle other people's bots and defeating them. To do so, one must program the behaviour of his <a href="wiki/Bot">bot</a> in our easy to learn <a href="wiki/Code">code</a>. The code will determine both bot's next moves, by using the given strategies. The bot with the best strategy will conquer!</p>
