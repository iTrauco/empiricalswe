<ul>
<li>External authentication system, such as Microsoft Exchange Server or any OAuth server.</li>
<li>External data stores. Allows you to mix a graph database, for the structure of the data, with a document store, for the contents of the data.</li>
<li>Big-data solutions can enabled analytics on large-scale historical data.</li>
</ul>
