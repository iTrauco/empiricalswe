<p><a href="http://www.sumatrapdfreader.org" rel="nofollow">SumatraPDF</a> is a PDF, EPUB, MOBI, CHM, XPS, DjVu, CBZ and CBR reader for Windows. It's small and starts up fast. Simplicity of the user interface is important to us</p>
<p>Download latest official release from <a href="https://www.sumatrapdfreader.org/download-free-pdf-viewer.html" rel="nofollow">download page</a>.</p>
<p>Or use frequently updated <a href="https://www.sumatrapdfreader.org/prerelease.html" rel="nofollow">pre-release builds</a> with latest, greatest features.</p>
<p>Found a bug? Want to request a feature? Use <a href="https://github.com/sumatrapdfreader/sumatrapdf/issues">issue tracker</a>.</p>
<p>Have feedback or questions? Discuss SumatraPDF in <a href="https://forum.sumatrapdfreader.org/" rel="nofollow">our forums</a>.</p>
<p>You can make SumatraPDF work better in your language by <a href="https://www.sumatrapdfreader.org/docs/How-to-contribute-translation-da023536e9774982b09937c8ed8cf6c1.html" rel="nofollow">improving translations of the program</a>.</p>
