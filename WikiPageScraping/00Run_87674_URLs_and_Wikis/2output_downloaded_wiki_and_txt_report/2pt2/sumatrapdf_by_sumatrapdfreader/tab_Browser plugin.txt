<p>In 3.0 we've removed browser plugin.
</p>
<p>Why?
</p>
<p>Chrome will completely remove support for NPAPI plugins (and Sumatra's plugin is based on NPAPI) by April 2015: [NPAPI](<a href="http://www.chromium.org/developers/npapi-deprecation" rel="nofollow">http://www.chromium.org/developers/npapi-deprecation</a>), and Firefox will remove support for most NPAPI plugins by the end of 2016: [NPAPI](<a href="https://blog.mozilla.org/futurereleases/2015/10/08/npapi-plugins-in-firefox/" rel="nofollow">https://blog.mozilla.org/futurereleases/2015/10/08/npapi-plugins-in-firefox/</a>)
</p>
<p>For those reasons we stopped including the plugin since version 3.0.
</p>
<p>That being said, we won't be deleting/disabling it, so if you really want it, you can install 2.5.2 from the [Previous](<a href="http://www.sumatrapdfreader.org/download-prev.html" rel="nofollow">http://www.sumatrapdfreader.org/download-prev.html</a>) and then upgrade, which will preserve the plugin.
</p>
