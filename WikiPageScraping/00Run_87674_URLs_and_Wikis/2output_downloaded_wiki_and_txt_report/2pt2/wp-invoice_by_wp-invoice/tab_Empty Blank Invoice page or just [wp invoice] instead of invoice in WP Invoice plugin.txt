        <div class="markdown-body">
          <p>First of all check following things:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Try to use "Turn off compatibility mode." option in Settings/Main tab.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Disable third-party plugins one by one to be sure that the issue is not related to any of them.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Try to switch theme to WordPress default(TwentyTwelve) to be sure that the issue is not related to the current theme.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>And contact your host provider. It can be that they do not allow get parameters to be paste.</p>
</li>
</ul>
