          <p>You will see following widgets in Appearance/Widgets tab after WP-Invoice plugin is activated.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Invoice Lookup</strong>. Allows you to add a simple invoice search form to a front-end. It will be accessible by your clients. Form accepts Invoice ID. Invoice page appears once correct Invoice ID submitted.</li>
<li>
<strong>Invoice History</strong>. Allows your clients to see a list of their invoices on a front-end.</li>
</ul>
