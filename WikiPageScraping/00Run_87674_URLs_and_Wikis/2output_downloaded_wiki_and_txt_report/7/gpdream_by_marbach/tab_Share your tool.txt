          <p><strong>Sharing your network inference tool on GP-DREAM is easy</strong>. It will greatly enhance your method's <strong>usability</strong>, <strong>visibility</strong> and <strong>impact</strong>.</p>
<p>Any command-line tool or script can be added (Java, C, R, Matlab, Python, etc). All that is needed is adapting the input and output file formats and the command line options for consistency with the other modules on GP-DREAM (see <strong><a class="internal present" href="/marbach/gpdream/wiki/Programmer-guide">Programmer guide</a></strong>). Methods are reviewed before a decision is taken to add them to the toolkit.</p>
<p><strong><a href="Contact">Contact us</a></strong> if you're interested in getting involved.</p>
