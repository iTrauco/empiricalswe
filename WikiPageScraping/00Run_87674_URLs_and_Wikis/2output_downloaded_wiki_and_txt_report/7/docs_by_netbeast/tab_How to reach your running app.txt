          <p>All apps in Netbeast router are launched in a random free port. To figure out in which port is running an app you just have to perform a <code>GET</code> request:</p>
<pre><code>GET http://home.netbeast/apps/{{your app name}}/port
</code></pre>
<p>Or simply interact with it through a proxy you have at:</p>
<pre><code>http://home.netbeast/i/{{your app name}}
</code></pre>
<p>So there is no need to know the port in advance.</p>
<p>Written with ♥ by Jesús D. Rivera.</p>
