          <p>You may skip this section if the GPIO pins are already active and ready for use on your RPi</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Update your RPi(just to be safe):
<code>sudo apt-get update</code></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Install the python development kit:
<code>sudo apt-get install python-dev</code></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Install the GPIO software:
<code>sudo apt-get install python-rpi.gpio</code></p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>You may go through a couple prompts. Just accept all of them.</p>
