          <p>The SteamKandy midi controller was designed to control the four supplemental loop decks available in <a href="http://www.mixxx.org" rel="nofollow">Mixxx DJ Software</a>. It also has four buttons to drop out/in the high and low for the two main decks. The controller can be easily redesigned and reprogrammed to fit your needs.</p>
<p>###Current Capabilities###</p>
<ul>
<li>Pause/Play four loop decks</li>
<li>Create and jump to two loop points</li>
<li>Loop deck volume</li>
<li>Drop in/out high and low for main two decks</li>
<li>Buttons light up on command received from Mixxx</li>
</ul>
<p>###Hardware###</p>

<li>Breadboard</li>
<li>LCD Screen</li>
<li><a href="https://www.adafruit.com/products/1477" rel="nofollow">16mm illuminated push buttons</a></li>
</ul>
