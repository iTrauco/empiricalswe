<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#what-is-this" id="user-content-what-is-this"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>What is this?</h1>
<p><em>Warning: This is still under editing</em></p>
<p>The primary goal of OpenFarm is to create an online database of plant and crop growing information freely accessible and usable by anyone.</p>
<p>We have a relatively clear idea of how we hope to achieve these goals, but as the project is a large one we know we won't be able to deliver everything at once.</p>
<p>This document aims to outline in more detail what our goals are, where we are now, where we want to be in the future, and how we get from A to B. This document will inevitably be a work in progress for quite some time!</p>
<h1>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#long-term-goals" id="user-content-long-term-goals"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Long Term Goals</h1>
<ol>
<li>Basics: It should be free, easy, educational, and convenient to share and read personal plant and crop growing guides from members around the world.</li>
<li>People should be able to add their own guides.</li>
<li>People should be able to save guides and relate them to their own garden.</li>
<li>People should be able to learn about all variety of crops and access details about those crops from a wide variety of open data sources.</li>
<li>Achieve long term goals in a safe and health working and contribution environment.</li>
<li>Anyone is capable of and is encouraged to contribute to the OpenFarm community, including but not limited to source code, financial contributions, guide listing, and community organizing.</li>
</ol>
