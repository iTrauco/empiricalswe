          <p>Tinkers Construct is an alternate way to generate tools and armor. The mod centers around the idea of smelting metal (turning it molten), then pouring it into molds to create parts. Every player starts out with a copy of the Tinker's Construct book Materials and You. You can create additional copies by using this recipe:</p>

<p><img alt="" data-canonical-src="http://sunkenlibrary.com/open/Rogue.Science.1.6.4/materials-and-you.png" src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/a401f6eef5c8673084c2ae4c3bb6a77b909c3a6c/687474703a2f2f73756e6b656e6c6962726172792e636f6d2f6f70656e2f526f6775652e536369656e63652e312e362e342f6d6174657269616c732d616e642d796f752e706e67"/></p>
<p>Tinker's construct tools cannot be enchanted, but their superior construction often makes them work like enchanted tools (faster, sharper, and more durable). There are tons of interesting (but entirely optional) new items and blocks available.</p>
<p>Like Thaumcraft, the content from Tinkers Construct is optional. You can still build normal tools the normal way.</p>

<p>Tinkers Construct adds a new tab to the normal inventory. This tab contains a number of new armor slots. It's worth noting, however, that only two of these slots are currently usable: The knapsack slot, and the Heart Container slot. Once you've manufactured a Knapsack, you can add a permanent second inventory to your character by placing it in the Knapsack slot.</p>
<p><img alt="" data-canonical-src="http://sunkenlibrary.com/open/Rogue.Science.1.6.4/Knapsack-placement.png" src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/7e812660d6ef867424dfca2c053a120a3f625f1b/687474703a2f2f73756e6b656e6c6962726172792e636f6d2f6f70656e2f526f6775652e536369656e63652e312e362e342f4b6e61707361636b2d706c6163656d656e742e706e67"/></p>
<p>The heart container slot can also be seen here (in the lower right). This slot can be used to add additional health to your character. The items for manufacturing heart containers are quite rare however, so don't expect find them early in the game.</p>
<p>DO NOT PLACE ITEMS IN THE OTHER SLOTS. These slots are unstable and shouldn't be used, they may cause the game to crash.</p>
