          <p>Thaumcraft adds an entire new magic system to Minecraft. The number of different things you can DO in Thaumcraft are far too numerous to list here. Fortunately, Thaumcraft has an entire system in place to aid your discovery of it's secrets.</p>
<p>To get started in Thaumcraft, you need two things: A bookshelf and a wand.</p>
<p>Bookshelves use the same recipe they've always used.</p>

<p>Once you have a wand, use it to right click on a bookshelf placed in the world. This will convert the bookshelf into a Thaumonomicon. The Thaumonomicon is your personal guidebook to the world and rules of Thaumcraft. Simply pick it up, place it in your hotbar, and right click to read. There's quite a bit to learn in here, but a dedicated player can unlock any number of extremely powerful or entertaining blocks, enchantments, or abilities.</p>
