<p>Getting started with the CTF-Platform is a simple and painless process. We have a recommended approach and will also post some F.A.Q. for people who are using other systems or hosts.</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#heroku" id="user-content-heroku"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Heroku</h2>
<p>Heroku is a free web hosting service that can deploy apps in many languages.</p>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#1-connect-with-heroku" id="user-content-1-connect-with-heroku"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>1. Connect with Heroku</h3>
<ol>
<li>Make an account on <a href="http://heroku.com" rel="nofollow">Heroku</a>
</li>
<li>Create a new app on Heroku.</li>
<li>Download the Heroku toolbelt.</li>
<li>Login to the Heroku toolbelt using <code>heroku login</code> and enter your credentials.</li>
</ol>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#2-clone-this-repo" id="user-content-2-clone-this-repo"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>2. Clone this repo</h3>
<ol>
<li>Navigate to the folder that you want to place the cloned repo in.</li>
<li><code>git clone https://github.com/EasyCTF/CTF-Platform.git</code></li>
</ol>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#3-deploy-to-heroku" id="user-content-3-deploy-to-heroku"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>3. Deploy to Heroku</h3>
<ol>
<li>Navigate into the repo folder.</li>
<li>
<code>heroku git:remote --app &lt;name&gt;</code> to add <code>heroku</code> as a remote. Replace <code>&lt;name&gt;</code> with whatever the name of the app you created in Step 1.2 was.</li>
<li>
<code>git push heroku master</code> to push this repo to Heroku. Note that it rebuilds the app every time you push.</li>
<li>
<code>heroku ps:scale web=1</code> to start running the <code>web</code> dyno.</li>
<li>
<code>heroku open</code> to open your app.</li>
</ol>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#4-get-a-database" id="user-content-4-get-a-database"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>4. Get a Database</h3>
<ol>
<li>On your Heroku dashboard, click your app name, and click Get Addons.</li>
<li>Look for a MongoDB addon, and add it to your app.</li>
<li>Once you get the credentials for the server, replace the part in <code>api/common.js</code> with your real information.</li>
<li>Push your changes to Heroku.</li>
</ol>
<h3>

Customize</h3>
<p>Change every instance of <code>CTF-Platform</code> to whatever the name of your CTF is going to be called.</p>
