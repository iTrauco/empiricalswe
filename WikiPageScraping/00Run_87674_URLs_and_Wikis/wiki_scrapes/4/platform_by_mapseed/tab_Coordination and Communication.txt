<p>There are several places where documentation and planning live:</p>
<ul>
<li>the <a href="https://github.com/mapseed/platform/wiki">GitHub wiki</a>, which has been sporadically updated in recent months; note specifically the <a href="https://github.com/mapseed/platform/wiki/Roadmap">roadmap</a>
</li>
<li>the <a href="http://wiki.smartercleanup.org/doku.php?id=start" rel="nofollow">SmarterCleanup wiki</a>, which was used in the past</li>
<li>the <a href="https://github.com/mapseed/platform/tree/master/doc"><code>docs</code> directory</a>, which is unchanged from the OpenPlans project, with just a few exceptions</li>
<li>the <a href="https://github.com/mapseed/platform/issues">GitHub issues</a> are used for short-term planning (specific technical changes, as well as slightly higher-level <a href="https://github.com/mapseed/platform/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3Ameta">meta issues</a>)</li>
<li>the <a href="https://github.com/mapseed/platform/projects">GitHub Projects page</a> is used somewhat interchangeably with meta issues</li>
</ul>
<p>These sources of information are largely out of sync with each other.</p>
