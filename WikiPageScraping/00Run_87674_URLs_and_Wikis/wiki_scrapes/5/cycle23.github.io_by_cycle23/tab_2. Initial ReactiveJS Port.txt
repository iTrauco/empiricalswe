<p>I've put up the first cut of the reactive-donkey Javascript.</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/cycle23/cycle23.github.io/tree/master/reactive-donkey">https://github.com/cycle23/cycle23.github.io/tree/master/reactive-donkey</a></p>
<p>index.html and donkey.js contain the bulk of the work.</p>
<p>The assets are expected to exist in purescript-magic parallel to this.</p>
<p>Those assets should probably be moved to some cdn, and I could also use bower more explicitly to avoid checking in the bower_components bit, but still evolving on that point.</p>
<p>Stopped just short of implementation of intersect for the coin, but otherwise near complete.</p>
<p>A lot of work trying to figure out a good way to get Intelli-J and bower to know about one another and also setup my html template and learning about 'let' and the use of the lambda/arrows "() =&gt; " anonymous function.</p>
<p>Those last bit force Firefox at the moment. I assume I could minify these later for cross platform or find substitute libraries or methods.</p>
<p>Also room here to use the underscore library instead of the home-brew assoc() method.</p>
