<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#installation-procedure" id="user-content-installation-procedure"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Installation Procedure</h3>
<ul>
<li>Connect EM power (12V direct or AC adapter to 110V)</li>
<li>Identify and connect peripheral cables (GPS, PSI, cameras, RFID) as applicable</li>
<li>Install blank hard drive.</li>
<li>Power on the EM box.</li>
<li>Ensure all signals green in UI.</li>
<li>Operate hydraulics to test PSI response.</li>
<li>Plugin Battery Leads</li>
<li>Confirm psi trigger delay</li>
<li>Copy a data sample to USB</li>
<li>Note HDD and Control box serial numbers. # m9</li>
<li>Set home port geometry, and verify video recording pauses at port.</li>
<li>Copy video samples and send to the team.</li>
<li>Set vessel timezone. Ensure time is being set correctly by GPS. <a href="https://github.com/Ecotrust-Canada/em-control-box/wiki/Servicing#setting-the-timezone">https://github.com/Ecotrust-Canada/em-control-box/wiki/Servicing#setting-the-timezone</a>
</li>
<li>Identify the best location for the three cameras and mount them to the vessel</li>
<li>Discuss the best placement for measuring strips with fisherman</li>
<li>Affix measuring strips</li>
<li>Fill in vessel information on VMP (attached)</li>
<li>Check the expected files are captured on the data drive.</li>
</ul>
