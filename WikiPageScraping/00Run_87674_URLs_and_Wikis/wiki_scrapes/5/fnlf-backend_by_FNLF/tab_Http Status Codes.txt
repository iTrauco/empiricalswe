<p>All responses without 500 will be of the format:</p>
<pre><code>{"_status": "ERR", "_error": {"message": "No HI for club 300-F", "code": 400}}
</code></pre>
<p><code>_status</code> will give an <code>ERR | OK?</code>, then the <code>_error</code> field will expand on that.</p>
<p>The server will send the http status code in it's response headers.</p>
