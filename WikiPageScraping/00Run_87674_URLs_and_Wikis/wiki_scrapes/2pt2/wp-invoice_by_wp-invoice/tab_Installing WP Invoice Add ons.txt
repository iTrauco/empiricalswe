<p>On the Licenses admin screen, you should see all your installed products, with an option to add your license key for product.</p>
<p><a href="https://storage.googleapis.com/media.usabilitydynamics.com/2014/10/8b31b7c7-adding-license.png" rel="nofollow"><img alt="" data-canonical-src="https://storage.googleapis.com/media.usabilitydynamics.com/2014/10/8b31b7c7-adding-license.png" src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/c0fae49c6852411d2c9b4a976c5e5103a89dd985/68747470733a2f2f73746f726167652e676f6f676c65617069732e636f6d2f6d656469612e75736162696c69747964796e616d6963732e636f6d2f323031342f31302f38623331623763372d616464696e672d6c6963656e73652e706e67"/></a></p>
<p>To <strong>add a license key</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>copy</strong> the license key from your <a href="https://usabilitydynamics.com/account" rel="nofollow">UD Account</a> page or receipt email.</li>
</ul>
<p><a href="https://storage.googleapis.com/media.usabilitydynamics.com/2014/10/0e51c3c9-copy-license.png" rel="nofollow"><img alt="copy license" data-canonical-src="https://storage.googleapis.com/media.usabilitydynamics.com/2014/10/0e51c3c9-copy-license.png" src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/62354925e582267b6add9ab3ad73e041cc305cda/68747470733a2f2f73746f726167652e676f6f676c65617069732e636f6d2f6d656469612e75736162696c69747964796e616d6963732e636f6d2f323031342f31302f30653531633363392d636f70792d6c6963656e73652e706e67"/></a></p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>paste</strong> the license key into input field for your product.</li>
<li>
<strong>save</strong> settings to <strong>activate</strong> your product.</li>
</ul>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#updating-your-product" id="user-content-updating-your-product"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Updating your product</h3>
<p>Once you have activated your product, you’ll be able to update your product via the <strong>Dashboard &gt; Updates</strong> admin screen.</p>
<p>To update your product:</p>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Dashboard &gt; Updates</strong>
</li>
<li>
<strong>Select</strong> your product under Plugins/Themes</li>
<li>Click the <strong>update</strong> button</li>
</ol>
<p>When updating plugins and themes, your files <strong>will be overwritten</strong>. We recommend <a href="http://codex.wordpress.org/Backing_Up_Your_WordPress_Files" rel="nofollow">backing up your files</a>, and using <a href="http://codex.wordpress.org/Child_Themes" rel="nofollow">child themes</a> for modifications.</p>

<p>Products do not have the limit of usage. License gives you the ability to update add-on or theme** within one year after download.** You will still have the ability to use a product after one year expired, but updates won’t be available anymore. To receive updates after one year expired you will need to redownload the product.</p>

<p>Please also note that we do not support our products if the code was customized directly. After update <strong>you will lose your plugin customization</strong>. If you need to customize some template in our plugin, you will need to copy the template to the root theme/child theme folder and only there customize it.</p>

<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#moving-licenses" id="user-content-moving-licenses"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Moving licenses</h3>
<p>Each add-on you can use on two sites. So if you need to test between staging and production it’s simple. You can also easily move licenses from site to site. To deactivate your license key and reactivate it for another site please go to your Account Dashboard &gt; <a href="https://usabilitydynamics.com/account" rel="nofollow">UD Account</a> page.</p>
<p>From here you can ‘deactivate’ your license key:</p>
<p><img alt="" data-canonical-src="https://storage.googleapis.com/media.usabilitydynamics.com/2014/10/66a7d124-deactivate-license.png" src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/49b463c0d841de27371fc4099cff695acd74cdf6/68747470733a2f2f73746f726167652e676f6f676c65617069732e636f6d2f6d656469612e75736162696c69747964796e616d6963732e636f6d2f323031342f31302f36366137643132342d646561637469766174652d6c6963656e73652e706e67"/></p>
<p>Then you can reactivate the same license by going to your other WordPress site’s Dashboard where it says WP-Property Licenses, and copy and paste your license and hit enter. Follow the steps above for Adding Licenses if needed. </p>
