<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#set-up--running-behat" id="user-content-set-up--running-behat"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Set up &amp; running Behat</h2>
<p>Create new behat.yml file that contains:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-yaml"><pre><span class="pl-ent">imports</span>:
    - <span class="pl-s">behat.dist.yml</span>

<span class="pl-ent">dev</span>:
    <span class="pl-ent">extensions</span>:
        <span class="pl-ent">Behat\MinkExtension</span>:
            <span class="pl-ent">base_url</span>: <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">'</span>http://mypage.com<span class="pl-pds">'</span></span></pre></div>
<p>Where <code>http://mypage.com/</code> is your developement host.
After that, just run</p>
<p><code>bin/behat --config behat.yml --profile dev</code></p>
<p>or for Windows</p>
<p><code>\bin\behat.bat --config behat.yml --profile dev</code></p>
