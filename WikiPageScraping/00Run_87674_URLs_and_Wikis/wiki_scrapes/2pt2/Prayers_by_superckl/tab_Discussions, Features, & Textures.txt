<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#altars" id="user-content-altars"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Altars</h3>
<blockquote>
<p>Altars will be multi-block structures. The altar must be constructed in a pre-defined shape (undetermined). The altars prayer points and recharge rate will be decided by what blocks are used to construct it. An activation ritual will be required. During this ritual, Undead Wizard Priests may spawn, increasing in strength along with the altar. Every altar must have at least one offering table. Once activated, the blocks will not be able to be broken by normal means. A player must activate a prayer that will let them destroy the altar. If the altar is modified in any way, it will need to be reactivated. The length and danger of the ritual will scale with the altar.</p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote>
<p>It'll always try to keep its distance from the player. Once it gets x blocks away, it'll fire rapidly. If it doesn't get interrupted, it'll change positions when it's done, keeping distance from the player and fire again. If the player interrupts it, it will either fly x blocks away, or poison/slow the player. If the player is poisoned/slowed, it will remain stationary for 3 seconds after the poison, then fly x blocks away. The 3 second window gives the player enough to get a couple swings off with the debuffs. To make level 3 and 4 more difficult, we'll have the wizard summon mobs via a portal, and fire more rapid, tracking orbs. They could have a shield that must be taken down prior to damaging it, and the shield regens after not being damaged for 7 seconds. And the poison cloud could heal any mobs in it.</p>
</blockquote>
