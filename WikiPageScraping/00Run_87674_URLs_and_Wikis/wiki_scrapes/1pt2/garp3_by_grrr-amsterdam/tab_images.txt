<p>In general, Garp uses different types of images.</p>
<ul>
<li>Uploaded images - uploaded by a user</li>
<li>Static images - used in templates</li>
<li>Interface images - referred to from css</li>
</ul>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#uploaded-images" id="user-content-uploaded-images"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Uploaded images</h2>
<p>These are not part of the codebase, but instead uploaded by a user, f.i. in the cms. They are generally stored in <code>(public)/uploads/images</code> on the local machine, or on Amazon S3. They also have a record in the database and they are relatable to other content. Their physical storage location is configured in <code>application/configs/assets.ini</code>.</p>
