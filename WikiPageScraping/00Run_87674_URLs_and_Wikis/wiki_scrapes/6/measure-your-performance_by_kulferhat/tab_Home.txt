          <p>Welcome to the measure-your-performance wiki!</p>
<p>Measure Your Computer Usage Performance, if it is a metric of :), number of keystrokes and number of mouse clicks on your daily computer work.
<a href="https://github.com/kulferhat/measure-your-performance/archive/master.zip">Download the project</a> and execute run.sh (for linux) or run.bat (for windows) under dist folder, and let program run on background while you work.</p>
<p>The program will count your keystrokes and mouse clicks and log total counts after each hour and day in myp.txt file under your home directory.</p>
<p>I guarantee, You will be surprised when you see counts :), and respect to keyboard and mouse because of their endurance.</p>
<p>A special thanks goes to <a href="https://github.com/kwhat/jnativehook">JNativeHook</a> for their library I used in this project to listen system to get keyboard and mouse events.</p>
