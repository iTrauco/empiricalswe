          <p>A path has two required parts, an owner and a directory. Optionally, it may contain a file.</p>
<pre><code>/brettin
/brettin/                 
/brettin/wsname           
/brettin/wsname/path
/brettin/wsname/path/file
           ^
           |
      sharable unit
</code></pre>
<p>The sharable unit can not be a file.
The thing following the username cannot be a file.
Sharing is set on the top level directory.</p>
