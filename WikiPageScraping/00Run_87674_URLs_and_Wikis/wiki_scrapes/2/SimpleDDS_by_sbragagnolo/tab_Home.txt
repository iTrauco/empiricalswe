          <p>SimpleDDS Is a Publisher / Subscriber (DDS) Architecture framework. First canon implementation of MetaDDS (Meta architecture for building specific DDS implementations).</p>
<p>SimpleDDS is meant to be ROS based, because the main reason of building it is for ROS interaction.
SimpleDDS is developed in Pharo 3.0 under TDD methodology, and it is daily built in <a href="https://ci.inria.fr/pharo-contribution/job/SimpleDDS" rel="nofollow">SimpleDDS CI</a>.</p>
<p>For downloading the project into your image you just need to execute</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Gofer it smalltalkhubUser: 'sbragagnolo'
project: 'SimpleDDS'; configuration;
loadVersion: #stable</p>
</blockquote>
<p>For learning about about the usage check in the pages, or in the comments of the Example package of the project.</p>
