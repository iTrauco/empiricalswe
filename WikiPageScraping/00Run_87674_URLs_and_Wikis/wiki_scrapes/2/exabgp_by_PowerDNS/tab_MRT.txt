          <p>MRT, Multi-Threaded Routing Toolkit (MRT) Routing Information Export Format, is a popular format when working on BGP updates. It is defined by <a href="https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc6396" rel="nofollow">RFC 6396</a></p>
<p>ExaBGP does not have yet any MRT support but some program may help you.</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/YoshiyukiYamauchi/mrtparse">mrtparse</a> will convert an MRT dump into an ExaBGP configuration.</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/lochiiconnectivity/ios2exa">IOS2EXA</a> convert Cisco IOS IPv4 BGP LOC Rib dumps to an ExaBGP configuration.</p>
