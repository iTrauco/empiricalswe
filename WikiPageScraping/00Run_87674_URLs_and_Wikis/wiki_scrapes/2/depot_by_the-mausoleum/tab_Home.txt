        <div class="markdown-body">
          <p>The desire to create Depot grew out of a working frustration with Microsoft Team Foundation Server (TFS), and how difficult it is to make modifications to a process template once a project has been created. Workflows evolve over time, and having to set one in stone from the beginning is asking for trouble.</p>
<p>The lofty goal of Depot is to be a working replacement for TFS, at least for small development teams.</p>
