          <p>.NET Core is transitioning from <em>project.json</em> and <em>.xproj</em> to the <em>.csproj</em> format. There are two relatively independent aspects here; the .NET Core runtime version and the project model. The table below lists the <em>generator-aspnet</em> version and the associated projects that are generated. For new projects, it's recommended that you use  <em>.csproj</em>  since that is the future of .NET Core.</p>
