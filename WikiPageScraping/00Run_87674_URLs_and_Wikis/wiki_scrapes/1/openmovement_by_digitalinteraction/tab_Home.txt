<p>The Open Movement project is a collection of hardware and software developed under open source terms with a variety of uses in mind, including: health research, digital interaction, instrumentation, gaming and music. With a global community of users including industry, universities and major health research organizations, Open Movement has fast being positioned as the de-facto standard for open source movement science.</p>
<p>All artefacts in this project including code, designs, software and analysis scripts tagged under the Open Movement umbrella are all completely free to use un-encumbered under the <a href="http://github.com/digitalinteraction/openmovement/blob/master/Software/LICENSE.TXT">BSD 2-clause license</a> and the <a href="http://github.com/digitalinteraction/openmovement/blob/master/Hardware/LICENSE.TXT">Creative Commons 3.0</a> By Attribution License. We firmly believe strong science comes through openness and sharing. Please feel free to use, distribute and share whatever you com across on these pages. If you wish to get more involved with the project, new contributors we are always welcome.</p>
