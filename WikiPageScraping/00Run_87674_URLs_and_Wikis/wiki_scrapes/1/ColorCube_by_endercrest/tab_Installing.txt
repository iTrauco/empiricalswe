<p><strong>Step one: Installation</strong></p>
<p>First, head over to the download page <a href="http://dev.bukkit.org/bukkit-plugins/colorcube/files/" rel="nofollow">here</a>. Now place the .jar into you plugins folder and restart your server. Make sure you have <a href="http://dev.bukkit.org/server-mods/worldedit/" rel="nofollow">WorldEdit</a> installed as well. ColorCube should now be installed. There a few configuration options in the config that you may change if you want.</p>
