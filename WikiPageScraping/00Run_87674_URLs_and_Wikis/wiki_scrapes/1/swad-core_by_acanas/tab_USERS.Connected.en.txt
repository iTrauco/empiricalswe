<h1>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#connected-users" id="user-content-connected-users"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Connected users</h1>
<p>The option <strong>USERS &gt; Connected</strong> displays information about open sessions and currently connected users.</p>
<p>A first box shows the following information:</p>
<ul>
<li>Number of sessions</li>
<li>Number of users
<ul>
<li>Number of students</li>
<li>Number of teachers</li>
<li>Number of guests</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>A second box shows a list of each of the users connected in the current scope.</p>
<ul>
<li>If your <a href="PROFILE.Session.en#role">current role</a> is administrator, it is possible to choose the scope.</li>
<li>If your <a href="PROFILE.Session.en#role">current role</a> is student or teacher within a course (scope = <em>current course</em>), it is not possible to select the scope and only the detailed list of connected users belonging to the current course is displayed.</li>
</ul>
<p>To update the list of connected users you can click the <em>Update</em> icon in the upper left corner of the box.</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#last-clicks" id="user-content-last-clicks"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Last clicks</h2>
<p>Clicking on the <strong>Last clicks</strong> link above the <em>Connected users</em> box will show a list with the last clicks or pageviews (with one click per row) and the following columns:</p>
<ul>
<li>Click number</li>
<li>Ago (minutes and seconds elapsed since the click was made)</li>
<li>Role</li>
<li>Country</li>
<li>Institution</li>
<li>Centre</li>
<li>Degree</li>
<li>Action</li>
</ul>
<p>The list is updated automatically in real time, with a refresh period of one second or less.</p>
