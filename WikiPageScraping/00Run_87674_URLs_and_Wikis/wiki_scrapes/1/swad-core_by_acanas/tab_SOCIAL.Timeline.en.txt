<h1>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#timeline" id="user-content-timeline"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Timeline</h1>
<p>The <strong>SOCIAL &gt; Timeline</strong> and <strong>SOCIAL &gt; Profiles</strong> options allow you to see a <em>timeline</em> (public activity chronologically ordered from the most recent to the oldest) in the style of other social networks (Twitter, Facebook...).</p>
<ul>
<li>In the <strong>SOCIAL &gt; Timeline</strong> option you will see the timeline of users that you <a href="SOCIAL.Profiles.en#follow-users">follow</a> or all users.</li>
<li>In the <strong>SOCIAL &gt; Profiles</strong> option you will see the timeline of a selected user.</li>
</ul>
<p>In the <strong>timeline</strong>, you will see:</p>
<ul>
<li>Public social posts</li>
<li>Comments on these messages</li>
</ul>
<p>and also links to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Public files.</li>
<li>Calls for exams.</li>
<li>Posts in <em>General</em> and <em>SWAD</em> forums.</li>
<li>Yellow notices.</li>
</ul>
<p>from the users you follow or from all the users.</p>
<p>It is possible to mention users in social posts and comments by writing their nicknames preceded by the @ symbol.</p>

<p>There are four types of notifications related to timeline:</p>
<ul>
<li>New comments to my social publishings</li>
<li>New favourite to my social publishings</li>
<li>New sharings of my social publishings</li>
<li>New mentions</li>
</ul>
