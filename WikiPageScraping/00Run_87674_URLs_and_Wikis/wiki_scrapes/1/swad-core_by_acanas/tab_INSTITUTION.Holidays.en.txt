<h1>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#holidays" id="user-content-holidays"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Holidays</h1>
<p>The <strong>Holidays</strong> icon at the upper left corner of the institution calendar (<a href="Calendar.en"><strong>INSTITUTION &gt; Calendar</strong></a>) allows you to view the holidays of the selected <a href="INSTITUTION.Information.en">institution</a>. A holiday may be:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Holiday</strong>: A single day. Written in red on the <a href="Calendar.en">calendar</a>.</li>
<li>
<strong>Non school period</strong>: A vacation period of one or more days. Written in purple on the <a href="Calendar.en">calendar</a>.</li>
</ul>
<p>You can sort the list of holidays by the name of the place or by start date (default option). To change the sorting criteria, click on the header of one of the two columns.</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#edit" id="user-content-edit"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Edit</h2>
<p>Institution administrators and system administrators can edit the holidays of the current institution by clicking on the <em>Edit</em> icon (pencil) in the upper left corner.</p>
<p>When you click on <em>Edit</em>, two boxes appear:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>New holiday</strong>. The first box allows you to <strong>add a new holiday</strong> by filling in the following columns and then clicking on the <em>Create holiday</em> button:
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Place</strong>. <em>All places</em> / one of the <a href="INSTITUTION.Places.en">places in the institution</a>.</li>
<li>
<strong>Type</strong>. <em>Holiday</em> (written in red on the <a href="Calendar.en">calendar</a>) / <em>Non school period</em> (written in purple on the <a href="Calendar.en">calendar</a>)</li>
<li>
<strong>Start date</strong>. Year, month and day.</li>
<li>
<strong>End date</strong>. Year, month and day. Same as start date when type is <em>Holiday</em>.</li>
<li>
<strong>Holiday</strong>. Name of the holiday. For example: "New Year's Day", "Christmas"...</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<strong>Holidays</strong>. The second box allows you to <strong>edit the existing holidays</strong>. You can change any of the columns.</li>
</ul>
<p>To <strong>remove</strong> a holiday, click on the <em>Remove</em> icon (red cross). No confirmation is required.</p>
