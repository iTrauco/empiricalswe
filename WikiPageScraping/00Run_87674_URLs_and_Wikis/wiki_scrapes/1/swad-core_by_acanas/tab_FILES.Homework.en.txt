<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#for-students" id="user-content-for-students"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>For students</h2>
<p>If you are a <strong>student</strong> in the course, the option <strong>FILES &gt; Homework</strong> will allow you to upload your works to these two areas:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Assignments area</strong>. This first zone of files called <em>Assignments</em> will allow you to upload files in the automatically created folders for each of the assignments that teachers have created in <a href="ASSESSMENT.Assignments.en">ASSESSMENT &gt; Assignments</a> with the upload option enabled, as long as the current date is within the range specified by the teacher for the assignment (the range of dates will be displayed in green).</li>
<li>
<strong>Homework area</strong>. This second zone of files called <em>Homework</em> will allow you to upload files at any time, regardless of deadlines.</li>
</ul>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#for-teachers" id="user-content-for-teachers"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>For teachers</h2>
<p>If you are a <strong>teacher</strong> in the course, the option <strong>FILES &gt; Homework</strong> will allow you to view and download the works uploaded by the students in the current course, as well as upload files for those areas of a particular student.</p>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#select-groups" id="user-content-select-groups"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Select groups</h3>
<p>If <a href="USERS.Groups.en"><strong>groups</strong></a> have been created in the current course, a box is displayed to select the groups of students whose homework you want to see. By default the groups to which you belong are selected. If you change this selection, you have to click on the link <em>Update students</em> to update the list of students to be shown below.</p>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#list-of-students" id="user-content-list-of-students"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>List of students</h3>
<p>Below is the <strong>list</strong> of students of the selected groups (or the entire course, if there are no groups). You can choose the presentation format of the students between:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>class photo</strong>, with students distributed in a two-dimensional matrix (default)
<ul>
<li>you can select the number of columns between 1 and 50, with 10 being the default</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<strong>list</strong>, with one student in each row
<ul>
<li>with photos (default) or without photos</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#select-students" id="user-content-select-students"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Select students</h3>
<p>Next to each student there is a <strong>checkbox</strong> that you can click to select that student. If you want to select all students, click on the <em>Students:</em> checkbox at the top of the list. If you want to deselect all students, click on the <em>Students:</em> checkbox again.</p>
<p>If you click on the button <strong>View homework</strong>, the homework of all the selected students will be displayed.</p>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#assignments-and-homework-areas" id="user-content-assignments-and-homework-areas"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Assignments and homework areas</h3>
<p>After clicking on <em>View homework</em>, a list with the selected students will be displayed. For each student it is shown:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Personal data</strong>. Student's photo, ID, name and email.</li>
<li>
<strong>Assignments area</strong>. Works uploaded by the student to the folders associated with the assignments created by teachers in <a href="ASSESSMENT.Assignments.en">ASSESSMENT &gt; Assignments</a> taking into account deadlines.</li>
<li>
<strong>Homework area</strong>. Works uploaded by the student without regard to deadlines.</li>
</ul>
<h3>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#zip-file" id="user-content-zip-file"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>ZIP file</h3>
<p>You can click on the <strong>Create ZIP file</strong> link to download all works in a single <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zip_(file_format)" rel="nofollow">ZIP file</a>. Within the ZIP file you will find a folder for each selected user, and within that folder, another two folders, one for the assignments zone and one for the homework zone.</p>
