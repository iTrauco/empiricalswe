<h1>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#announcements-of-exams" id="user-content-announcements-of-exams"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Announcements of exams</h1>
<p>In <strong>ASSESSMENT &gt; Announcements</strong> teachers can create announcements of exams for the current course. The announcements are listed in descending order of date, from the most recent to the oldest. Each of the announcements is shown in the <a href="Calendar.en">calendar</a> as an event in orange with a link to the announcement.</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#new-announcement" id="user-content-new-announcement"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>New announcement</h2>
<p>To <strong>create</strong> a new announcement, you must click on the <em>New announcement</em> icon (plus sign) at the top left of the box or the button <em>New announcement</em> at the end of the list. A form appears with the following fields:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Course</strong>. By default this field appears filled with the full name of the course, but you can edit it.</li>
<li>
<strong>Year or semester</strong>. N/A, 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th,...</li>
<li>
<strong>Session</strong>. Ordinary, extraordinary, partial, final, June, September,...</li>
<li>
<strong>Exam date</strong>. Date (year, month and day) on which the exam will take place.</li>
<li>
<strong>Start time</strong>. Time (hour and minute) for the exam to take place. Unlike other dates and times on the platform, which are displayed in the user's local time zone, exam announcements always show the dates and times that teachers wrote, without adjusting them to the local time zone of the user.</li>
<li>
<strong>Approximate duration</strong>. Approximate duration of the exam expressed in hours and minutes.</li>
<li>
<strong>Place of exam</strong>. Physical place (classroom, laboratory) or virtual (platform, web) in which the exam will take place.</li>
<li>
<strong>Mode</strong>. Written, spoken,...</li>
<li>
<strong>Structure of the exam</strong>. Detailed description of the examination structure, including each of its parts and the duration of each part.</li>
<li>
<strong>ID required</strong>. Personal documents required to prove that the student is who he or she claims to be (ID, passport, ...).</li>
<li>
<strong>Material required</strong>. Notes, books, pen, pencil, calculator,... which are mandatory to take to the exam.</li>
<li>
<strong>Material allowed</strong>. Notes, books, pen, pencil, calculator,... which can optionally be taken to the exam.</li>
<li>
<strong>Other information</strong>. Any other information for the students that the teachers consider opportune to clarify.</li>
</ul>
<p>When you fill in the fields, you must press the button <em>Publish announcement</em>. The new announcement will be listed and a link to it will be created in the <a href="Calendar.en">calendar</a>.</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#edit-announcement" id="user-content-edit-announcement"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Edit announcement</h2>
<p>To <strong>remove</strong> an existing announcement, click on the <em>Remove</em> icon (red cross). To effectively remove the announcement you will have to confirm the deletion by clicking on the <em>Remove announcement</em> button.</p>
<p>To <strong>hide</strong> an existing announcement, click on the <em>Hide</em> icon (eye). To <strong>unhide</strong> a hidden announcement, click the <em>Unhide</em> icon (strikethrough eye).</p>
<p>To <strong>edit</strong> an existing announcement, click on the <em>Edit</em> icon (pencil). A form identical to the one for <a href="#new-announcement">creating a new announcement</a> will appear. When the editing is finished, click on the <em>Publish announcement</em> button.</p>
<h2>
<a aria-hidden="true" class="anchor" href="#print-announcement" id="user-content-print-announcement"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z" fill-rule="evenodd"></path></svg></a>Print announcement</h2>
<p>To <strong>print</strong> an announcement, click on the <em>Print</em> icon. The announcement of exam will be displayed ready to be printed on a new page. At the end of the announcement, a blank space is left in case the teachers want to sign. Below the call is a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/QR_code" rel="nofollow">QR code</a>, which students can scan with their smart phones to access the announcement in the platform.</p>
