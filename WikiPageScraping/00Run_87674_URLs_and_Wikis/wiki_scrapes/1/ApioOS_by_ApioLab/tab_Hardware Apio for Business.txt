          <p>###NETWORK MESH</p>
<p>A mesh network is a network topology in which each node relays data for the network and cooperate in its distribution. A mesh network must have one coordinator device, tasked with its creation, control and basic maintenance, along with one or more dislocated nodes.</p>

<p>##APIO DIN8</p>
<p>Apio DIN8 is the biggest of the Apio DIN family and like the others hardware is designed to facilitate the digitalization of the electrical systems by centralizing the data from plant lines (power, energy, electricity, gas, water consumption, etc.). It allows you to make actuations through a Wireless Mesh Network created by Apio Gateway that sync with ApioOS in Cloud.
Apio DIN8 easily interfaces with many energy meter and inverter such as Seneca, Eastron, Huawei and Selco.
This manual explains how to connect sensors and / or actuators to the Apio DIN8.</p>
