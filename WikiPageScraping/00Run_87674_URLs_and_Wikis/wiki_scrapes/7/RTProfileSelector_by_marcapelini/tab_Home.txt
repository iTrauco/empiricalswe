          <p><strong>RTProfileSelector</strong> is a <a href="http://rawtherapee.com/" rel="nofollow">RawTherapee</a> plugin that automatically selects <em>custom processing profiles</em> (RawTherapee's presets, stored as ".pp3" files) based on user-defined rules. The rules are sets of Exif fields and values which are matched against the actual values extracted from the raw files the first time they are opened in RawTherapee.</p>
<p>The idea is that you can create your own presets, and then have RawTherapee use them automatically based on any combination of Exif fields (i.e. "rules") which you see fit!</p>
<p>A few things you can automate in RawTherapee through <em>RTProfileSelector</em>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assign your own custom processing profiles to approximatelly match your camera settings (such as "monochrome"/"black &amp; white", "vivid colour", "film modes", etc.)</li>
<li>Set noise reduction parameters in RawTherapee according to camera model and ISO value</li>
<li>Assign lens correction profiles (LCP) based on the lens and camera model used</li>
</ul>
