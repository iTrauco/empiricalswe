<br/>
<p><a href="https://getyeti.co/" rel="nofollow"><img alt="Netbeast" src="https://github.com/netbeast-co/router/raw/master/img/full-logo.png?raw=true"/></a></p>
<p><strong>Netbeast</strong> is an operative system that allows you to control different smart devices under a common control system favouring the communication between them regardless of their brand, the technology they use or how they work.</p>
<p>Join the community if you want to know more about the project! Visit <a href="http://bit.ly/1FfOLZS" rel="nofollow">Netbeast</a></p>
<p>Also, go to <strong>Downloads</strong> and <strong>Developer</strong> sections and put your developments skills to test!</p>
<p>If you want to know, why you have to choose Netbeast OS, go here <a href="https://github.com/netbeast-co/docs/wiki/Why-Netbeast">Why Netbeast?</a></p>
<p><a href="https://getyeti.co/" rel="nofollow"><img alt="Netbeast" src="https://github.com/netbeast-co/router/raw/master/img/works-with-yeti.png?raw=true"/></a></p>
<p>Netbeast has developed an application based on this open source project called <strong>Yeti</strong>.
The app is available on the <a href="http://m.onelink.me/4e70ec81" rel="nofollow">AppStore</a> for iOS and also on the <a href="http://m.onelink.me/444eef11" rel="nofollow">PlayStore</a> for Android devices</p>

<p>This is an Operative system based on Linux Distribution for RPI. You can install this operative system to your raspberry pi and you will have our <strong>Netbeast Dashboard</strong> running on your RPI ready to control all your smart home devices. If you want to know how to get it or you want to know more about this project, go here:</p>
