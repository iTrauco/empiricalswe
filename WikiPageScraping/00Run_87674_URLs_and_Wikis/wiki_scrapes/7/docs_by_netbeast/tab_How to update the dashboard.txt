          <ol>
<li>Log in to your Netbeast router, wether it is through <a href="http://home.netbeast" rel="nofollow">http://home.netbeast</a> and opening the terminal app or through <code>ssh root@home.netbeast</code>.</li>
<li>Type:</li>
</ol>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-shell"><pre>npm -g install nb-dashboard
service dashboard stop
service dashboard start</pre></div>
<p>Done!</p>
<p>It is expected to be a feature of following releases to do this automatically for you by just pressing a button or automatically with each new release.</p>

<ul>
<li>Folders in config.json are not created after installation, due to <code>.gitignore</code>. To fix this go to nb-dashboard folder (most probably in <code>/lib/node_modules/nb-dashboard</code> or <code>/usr/lib/node_modules/nb-dashboard</code> and run <code>mkdir sandbox; mkdir tmp</code>.</li>
</ul>
