          <p>When a search is saved you give it a name, and it is stored in your browser's local storage. Saved searches are not portable because they do not save in a database or any other "central" location. You may find that you need to delete a saved search. This screen lets you do just that.</p>
<p><img alt="Manage Saved Searches" src="images/manage-saved-searches.png"/></p>
<p>This screen will display a card for each search you have saved. They contain the criteria for the search, including the subject/message, from, and to filters. If you no longer need a saved search simply click on the <strong>Delete</strong> button on the card that contains the saved search you wish to remove.</p>
